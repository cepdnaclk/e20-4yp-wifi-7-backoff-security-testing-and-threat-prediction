**How It Works:**

- Wi-Fi 7 integrates TSN features to support:

    - **Scheduled traffic** (IEEE 802.1Qbv).
        
    - **Low latency and jitter control**.

**Why It Matters:**

- Enables Wi-Fi 7 to support **industrial automation**, **real-time robotics**, and **vehicular communications**.
    
- Critical step in **wireless determinism**.
