## **Backward Compatibility**

### **What it is:**

- Wi-Fi 7 devices can still communicate with **Wi-Fi 6, 6E, 5, and earlier** devices.
    

### **How it works:**

- Uses **legacy preambles and signaling** when communicating with older devices.
    

### **Pros:**

- Smooth **network upgrade path**.
    
- Older devices continue to function.
    

### **Cons:**

- Maintaining legacy support can slightly **reduce peak efficiency** in mixed networks.
