## **Low Latency & High Reliability Features**

### **Target Wake Time (TWT) Enhancements:**

- Devices schedule when to wake up for transmissions.
    
- Reduces **contention** and **power consumption**.
    

### **QoS (Quality of Service) Enhancements:**

- Prioritizes **time-sensitive traffic** like VR/AR, gaming, and real-time video.
    

### **Pros:**

- **Reduced latency for critical applications**.
    
- **Power-efficient** for battery-powered devices.
    

### **Cons:**

- Advanced scheduling requires **support from both AP and clients**.
    

### **Use cases:**

- VR/AR streaming.
    
- Online gaming with very low ping requirements.
    
- Industrial IoT networks.
