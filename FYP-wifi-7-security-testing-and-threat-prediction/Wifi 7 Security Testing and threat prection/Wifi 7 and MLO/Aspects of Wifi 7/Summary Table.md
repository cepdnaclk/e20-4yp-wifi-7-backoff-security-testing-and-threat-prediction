| Feature                | Benefit                              | Drawback                              | Use Case                  |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| 320 MHz Channels       | Ultra-high throughput                | Shorter range, interference-sensitive | 8K streaming, VR          |
| 4096-QAM               | More bits per symbol → faster data   | High SNR needed                       | Gigabit Wi-Fi, enterprise |
| MLO                    | Multi-band simultaneous transmission | Complex scheduling                    | Gaming, cloud apps        |
| MU-MIMO & OFDMA        | More users at once                   | Device complexity                     | Stadiums, smart factories |
| TWT & QoS              | Low latency, power saving            | Needs client support                  | VR/AR, gaming, IoT        |
| Preamble Puncturing    | Resilient to interference            | Slight throughput reduction           | Dense urban networks      |
| Backward Compatibility | Smooth upgrade                       | Slight efficiency loss                | Mixed device networks     |
| Spectral Efficiency    | Max throughput per Hz                | Complexity                            | High-density networks     |
- Wi-Fi 7 is **all about speed, reliability, and efficiency**.
    
- It’s ideal for **dense, high-bandwidth, low-latency environments**.
    
- Many of its features (like 4096-QAM and MLO) **require strong signals and advanced hardware**.
